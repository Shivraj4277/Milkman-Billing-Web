About Us

Shree Mari-Aai Dodha Sanstha Karnur is a trusted milk and dairy delivery service from Karnur. With over a decade of experience in dairy distribution, we are committed to bringing pure, farm-fresh milk to your doorstep every morning.

We offer fresh cow and buffalo milk, along with other dairy products such as curd, ghee, and butter. Our delivery is always timely (5:00 AM to 7:00 AM), hygienic, and tailored to your subscription needs.

We believe in transparency, customer satisfaction, and long-term trust. Whether you are an individual household or a business, our dairy service can be customized to your requirement.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fresh and pure milk directly from local farms
  • Timely delivery every morning
  • Affordable and transparent pricing
  • Easy subscription and billing system
  • Friendly and experienced delivery team
